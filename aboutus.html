About Us

Founded in 2016, The Perfume Shop set out with the simple idea of making luxury perfume brands accessible to everyone at an affordable price. With the first store openings in Birmingham, Belfast & Milton Keynes, our Perfume Shop developed a genuine passion for delighting customers with expert perfume knowledge and the highest customer service standards which remain at the forefront of everything the brand stands for today. In 2005 our Perfume Shop was acquired by the world's largest international health and beauty retailer A.S Watson - known in 25 markets, they opened their 15,000th store in March 2019.
